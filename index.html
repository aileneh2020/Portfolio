About Me

While taking college courses, I was introduced to Java, C++ and Visual Basic. That was when I developed a great interest in coding. Although my career went a different path, my passion for coding never went away.

In 2018, I received an opportunity from Udacity to earn a Front-End Web Developer Nanodegree through the Grow with Google scholarship program. Of all applicants nationwide, 15,000 were selected to participate in the challenge course which was 3 months of HTML, CSS and JavaScript basics. I was among the top 10% of students and earned a scholarship to complete the full course which was another 6 months of intense learning and projects. I am proud to have completed this course and earn my Front-End Web Developer Nanodegree.

Since then, I have completed another course on React JS. My current project is building a website for a client using WordPress.
